The Cost of Poor Quality

Most bugs are cheapest the day before release — and most expensive the day after.

Untested software doesn't fail quietly. It fails in front of customers. Here's what I help teams avoid:

🐛

Escaped Defects

Bugs found in production cost far more to fix than in testing → lost user trust

🔁

Regression Roulette

Manual-only testing means every release risks breaking what worked → slow, fearful shipping

📉

Untraceable Quality

Without clear defect reports and coverage, no one knows what's actually tested → blind releases

The Process

A simple, proven 3-step testing workflow.

Turning requirements into confidence — sidestepping the pitfalls that let bugs escape.

01 · ANALYZE

Understand & Plan 🔍

Study requirements, map risk areas, and design test cases that cover the paths users actually take — including the ones nobody expects.

02 · AUTOMATE

Build & Execute ⚙️

Automate the repetitive with Selenium and Postman, run regression suites on every change, and validate data end-to-end with SQL.

03 · ASSURE

Report & Ship 🚀

Document defects with crystal-clear reproduction steps, track them through resolution, and sign off releases with evidence — not hope.
